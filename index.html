PARK RULES

To ensure everyone has a safe and enjoyable experience at HAVOK, please read and follow the rules below: 1. Protective gear must be worn at all times when riding. We strongly recommend a full-face helmet and knee pads as a minimum.

2. Full-face helmets and kneepads are mandatory for all riders under 16.

3. Under 18s must have a parent or guardian sign their disclaimer form before riding the park.

4. Under 14s must always be accompanied by a parent or guardian while riding at the park.

Find us

We are located in Todmorden in the north of the UK. To get to us, turn onto Station Parade and follow the road to the railway crossing. You must exit your vehicle and press the button on the barriers to lift them.
