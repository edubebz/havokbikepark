HAVOK BIKE PARK

HAVOK bike park is back, and better than ever. Since the closure of the previously well loved park, Sam and the team have worked tirelessly to bring you a BRAND new, refined and improved park!
We have a range of trails to suit all abilities, from beginner to expert. Above all else, we value community and riding experience and the art of crafting trails with over 30 years of combined building experience.

We are proud to be a rider owned and operated park and cant wait to welcome you to the new HAVOK.
