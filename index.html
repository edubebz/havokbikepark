HAVOK IS BACK

HAVOK bike park is back, and better than ever. Since the closure of the previously well loved park, Sam and the team have worked tirelessly to bring you a BRAND new, refined and improved park!
We have a range of trails to suit all abilities, from beginner to expert. Above all else, we value community and riding experience and the art of crafting trails with over 30 years of combined building experience.

We're working tirelessly to add new trails, features and improvements every week so keep an eye on our Facebook and Instagram for updates!

We are proud to be a rider owned and operated park and cant wait to welcome you to the new HAVOK.

Pricing

We offer pay on the day passes and yearly passes for those who love riding as much as us. This allows you to ride the park whenever you like, even outside of weekend hours - Get the park to yourself!

Find us

We are located in Todmorden in the north of the UK, to get to us, turn onto Station Parade and follow the road to the railway crossing, you must exit your vehicle and press the button on the barriers to lift them.


!!! YOU MUST CLOSE THE BARRIER BEHIND YOU !!!


Parking is available on site, take a left just after the barriers and a second immediate left!
