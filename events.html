HAVOK BIKE PARK RACE WEEKEND

Finishing the event season with race vibes!
Saturday 20th - Sunday 21st September 2025
We’re mixing up sections of our original trails to create a fresh downhill-style line that’s rollable yet challenging. Expect flow, speed, and a few smaller features to keep things spicy – you’ll need to keep your head in the game to pick the best lines! The track will stay completely secret until the weekend, with your first chance to ride it on Saturday during practice.

This will be a mates race – meaning while there’s still a competitive edge, the focus is on a fun, supportive atmosphere where everyone can push themselves and have a laugh with their mates.

Hope WMN x Melon Optics Women’s Day - AUG 2ND!

Join us for a full day of riding, community, and celebration at the Hope WMN x Melon Optics Women’s Day, hosted at Havok Bike Park by Hope WMN, Melon Optics, and the Havok crew. This event is all about championing women in the mountain bike industry — whether you race, ride for fun, or just love the culture.

✨ Note: This isn’t a women-only event — everyone is welcome to ride or support — but the day is designed to uplift and celebrate the incredible women of MTB.
