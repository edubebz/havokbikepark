FIRE ROAD

The fire road is your key to the park, it is an average 10 minute push up to the top, or 3-5 minute ride on an e-bike! Access is linked directly to the park hub and all trails lead here. Please be aware that the uplift also uses this road - please give way to any uplifts running up or down here safely.

Jammy Lasagne - RED Run

This RED-rated bike park trail is perfect for intermediate riders. Starting at the top fork, it splits right, leading into a flowy descent with well-graded, rollable features. The trail has accessible tables that cater to all skill levels, allowing riders to confidently clear or roll over them. The ride concludes with a large, inviting table jump, bringing you smoothly back to the central hub, ready for another lap!

The Savage Cabbage - DOUBLE BLACK Run

One of our OG trails with tight steep turns, our signature Y shaped tree gap and renowned drop into step up combo, finishing with a sharkfin into booter.
This track starts at the very top and splits off to the left quickly. After an initial step up, it takes you through the steep twists and turns of our forest area, through the legendary Y tree gap before leading out to some huge berms. Our huge sheep skull drop waits for you, taking you with the perfect speed into the famous step up, ending with an epic sharkfin gap into a final step up. Whilst the first half of the trail before the Y tree is rollable, the second half contains mandatory features that should be walked before riding!

Sundried Mum - BLACK run

This BLACK-rated pro trail is designed for advanced riders seeking big air and technical challenges. It kicks off with two massive sharkfins that launch you into perfect setup for a very big table jump. Next, the trail flows into an on-off lily pad feature, requiring precision and control, before sending you over a final substantial gap jump. The adrenaline continues as the trail transitions into a fast, downhill-style descent with technical sections and steep drops. The trail finishes strong, leading you back to the hub, ready for your next thrill ride.

Dirty Jumps - RED & PRO Jumps/mulch booter

This new dirt jump zone offers two parallel lines catering to different skill levels. The advanced line features larger jumps, delivering big air and challenging transitions, while the smaller line is perfect for those newer to dirt jumping, with more forgiving takeoffs and landings. Both lines are short, with a quick push-up back to the start, allowing for easy repeatability. The zone culminates in a choice of five different jumps, offering diverse styles and difficulty levels. A standout feature is the massive mulch trick jump, accessible as a solo line and designed for safely learning and practicing new tricks with a soft, forgiving landing.

Jewlo Jank - Dual Slalom Track

This dual slalom track is tight, steep, and built for head-to-head racing with friends or rivals. With its sharp turns and steep tech, it demands precision and speed, making every race a thrilling challenge. Frequently used in park-hosted events, this track is a favorite for competitive riders looking to test their skills and claim victory.
