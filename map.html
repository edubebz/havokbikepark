FIRE ROAD

The fire road is your key to the park, it is average 10 minute push up to the top, or 3 minute ride on an e-bike! Access is linked directly to the park hub and all trails lead here.

The Savage Cabbage - Red Run

One of our OG trails with tight steep turns, our signature Y shaped tree gap and renown drop into step up combo.
This track starts at the very top and splits off to the left quickly. After an initial step up, it takes you through the steep twists and turns of our forest area, through the legendary Y tree gap before leading out to some huge berms. Our huge sheep skull drop waits for you, taking you with the perfect speed into the famous step up, ending with an epic sharkfin gap into a final step up. Whilst the first half of the trail before the Y tree is rollable, the second half conatains mandatory features that should be walked before riding!
